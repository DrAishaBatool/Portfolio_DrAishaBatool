About Me

I am a dedicated academic and researcher with a PhD in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad. With over 7 years of teaching and research experience, my work focuses on machine learning, computer vision, and blockchain-based systems. I am passionate about solving real-world problems through advanced technologies and contributing to impactful academic research. Currently, I serve as a Lecturer at the University of Wah and have published more than a dozen peer-reviewed articles in reputed journals and conferences.
